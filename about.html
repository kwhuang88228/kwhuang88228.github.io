MIT CSAIL

I’m a Master’s student at MIT CSAIL advised by Professor Bill Freeman, interested in computer vision, graphics, and NLP. In particular, I’m passionate about problems in self-supervised multimodal learning, neural feature fields, and representation learning. I’m also excited to apply machine learning methods to address robotics, semiconductors, medical, and geoscience challenges. I graduated from MIT with a S.B. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science in June 2021 and worked with Professor Antonio Torralba and Professor Dimitrios Papadopoulos. I’m a basketball fanatic, chess lover, licensed lifeguard, and 60 Minutes enthusiast. I’m also committed to volunteer in all stages of my life.
